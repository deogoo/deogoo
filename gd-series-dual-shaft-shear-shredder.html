GD Series Dual-Shaft Shear Shredder

Published on March 14, 2025

In today's era of pursuing high efficiency and environmental protection, the GD series dual-shaft shear shredder, with its unique design and technical advantages, has become the ideal choice for urban waste disposal, resource recycling, and pre-treatment of waste incineration. This shredder features a "dual motor + dual planetary gearbox" power system, which not only provides strong power output but also ensures high stability during operation. The low-speed and high-torque design gives it immense shear force, effectively shredding various materials while maintaining a low working noise level.

One notable feature of the GD series is its integrated casing design, which not only improves the precision of the equipment but also enhances the overall stability and durability of the machine. The knife discs are made from imported materials, which increase hardness while reducing brittleness, significantly extending the lifespan of the cutting tools. Additionally, users can choose between hydraulic motor or electric motor drive systems based on their specific needs. The hydraulic motor is particularly suitable for operations in harsh environments, while the electric motor offers a more energy-efficient option. To further improve the durability of the equipment, the entire casing undergoes heat treatment to effectively prevent corrosion and wear, thereby extending its service life.

The machine can be driven by either an electric motor or a hydraulic motor. The hydraulic motor is ideal for harsher working environments, offering stepless speed control, forward and reverse rotation. When encountering uncrushable materials, the motor can reverse to re-shred, protecting the machine from damage.

Intelligent technology is one of the key trends in the development of modern machinery, and the GD series dual-shaft shear shredder is no exception. It is equipped with an advanced intelligent control system that allows users to monitor the machine's working status in real-time via mobile phones or computers. This system not only monitors the real-time temperature of each component, triggering an alarm or automatic shutdown to protect the equipment if abnormal temperatures are detected, but it also allows the machine to automatically reverse or stop when material blockages occur, preventing damage. The main shaft is processed using heat treatment technology, increasing its mechanical strength and further ensuring the long-term stable operation of the equipment.

In the field of modern waste management and resource recycling, the high-efficiency shredding equipment we offer can handle a wide range of complex material processing needs. Whether it's the disposal of old sofa mattresses, waste circuit boards, fiberglass, rotten waste, and landfill trash, or the shredding of scrapped cars and their parts, used rubber, old furniture, and the harmless treatment of dead animals, as well as the processing of wet waste, dry waste, discarded home appliances, wooden waste, and biomass straw, our shredders provide reliable solutions. Through advanced shredding technology, we can effectively reduce the volume of waste materials, making them easier for subsequent transportation and processing. We also promote resource recycling, contributing to environmental protection. Choosing our shredder means choosing a highly efficient, environmentally friendly waste processing partner.
