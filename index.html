About Us

ZHENGZHOU GEP ECOTECH Co., Ltd, which belongs to the GEP Group, is an entity-oriented enterprise driven by both technological innovation and social responsibility. Relying on the strong support of the two R&D centres GEP ECOTECH in North American and in China, we continue to export high-energy and low-consumption shredders, crushers, screens and other supporting equipment for solid waste disposal and recycling, and provide solutions with strong availability.
